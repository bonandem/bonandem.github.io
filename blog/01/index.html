Welcome to Our Blog!

Oct 15 - Written by Bonnie and Emily


We are Bonnie and Emily, two friends that came together through our love for food, travel, and design.

Our blog is a hodgepodge of all the things we love and want to share with others. From cooking to traveling to restaurants and all other shenanigans in our lives, we hope our content will build a community of friends who all share similar interests.

For the past 10 years, we have spent countless hours cooking, baking, and eating together. Our journey began back in 2012 when we first met in high school where we shared our love for baking and cooking. One of the very first few creations we made were lemon bars in a makeshift tin foil pan. It was in these little moments of spontaneity and chaos where we bonded the most.

Flash forward a few years and we are now in New York City exploring, eating and spending too many sleepless nights studying together. These few years in NYC hold many memories and adventures we hope to share with you all very soon. From our travels to Hong Kong, Japan, and Thailand to just exploring the boroughs of New York City, we have much to share about where we went, what we ate, and so much more. Not only will we be sharing the adventures we have been on together, we will also be sharing some of our own travels and experiences as well.

We hope you enjoy this sneak peek and will keep coming back for more!


Thanks for checking out our blog!
